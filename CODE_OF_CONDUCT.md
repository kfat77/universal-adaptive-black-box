# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to a respectful, harassment-free environment for everyone who participates in this project.

## Expected Behavior

- Be constructive, respectful, and considerate of different backgrounds and experience levels.
- Focus criticism on ideas, code, experiments, and documentation rather than people.
- Respect privacy and do not publish private data, credentials, or sensitive experimental material.

## Unacceptable Behavior

Harassment, discrimination, personal attacks, intimidation, deliberate disruption, or sharing private information without permission are not acceptable.

## Reporting

Please report concerns privately through the repository security contact or a GitHub issue when safe to do so. Maintainers may remove comments, close contributions, or restrict participation to protect the community.
